## ‚ùì QUESTIONS?

**Q: How many blog posts until I see results?**
A: Usually 5-10 posts. First results in 2-4 weeks for long-tail keywords.

**Q: Should I focus on blog or video first?**
A: Blog posts rank faster (2-4 weeks). Videos take 4-8 weeks but last longer. Do both if possible.

**Q: What if my first posts don't rank?**
A: Normal! Keep publishing. Google needs to see you're consistent (not a one-hit wonder).

**Q: How much time per week?**
A: Minimum:
- 3 hours/week for blog writing
- 1 hour/week for social media
- 1 hour/week for link building

**Q: When should I hire help?**

- Content writer ($50-100/article)
